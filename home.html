Programming languages are tools that humans use to create software and automate tasks.
They are good for humans in several ways:
  • Empowering creativity: Programming languages allow people to express their creativity and solve problems in new and innovative ways. They provide a way for people to bring their ideas to life and create something that is uniquely their own.
  • Boosting productivity: Programming languages make it easier for humans to automate repetitive or tedious tasks. This saves time and allows people to focus on more important tasks, leading to increased productivity.
  • Enhancing problem-solving skills: Programming languages require people to think logically and break down complex problems into smaller, more manageable pieces. This helps develop problem-solving skills that can be applied to other areas of life.
